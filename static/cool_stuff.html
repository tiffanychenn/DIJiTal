Both players roll the die by pressing the "Roll Die" button. The game will move you automatically.
Player 1 uses the w key to start the slot machine and then stops the left, middle, and right columns with the a, s, and d keys, respectively. Player 1 uses the i key to start the slot machine and then stops the left, middle, and right columns with the j, k, and l keys, respectively.
