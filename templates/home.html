About:

Two players will play on the same computer for 10 turns. Each player starts with 20 coins. In every turn, each player will roll the die and land on a spot where they will gain/lose a certain amount of coins. After each player traverses the board, a minigame will randomly be selected for them to play. The winner of the minigame will receive 10 coins. The player with the most coins at the end wins.
